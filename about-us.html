About Vikash Srivastava

Vikash Srivastava is Associated with MOTILAL OSWAL SECURITIES LTD as sub-broker / business partner since 2009.

Motilal Oswal Securities Ltd is having 30yrs of wealth creation experience and is Indias one of the best performing equity brokers.

With the humble beginning, today Vikash Srivastava has blossomed into a well diversified company offering a wide range of financial products and services, such as broking and distribution, research and advisory and training and consultancy.

Our aim is to be a facilitator in the journey of your wealth creation.

Our client base includes retail customers, HNIs and Corporates.

As a customer you can trade in equity, derivatives, commodities, currency, mutual funds, IPOs, bonds and insurance. We trade in BSE, NSE, NCDEX, and MCX through various platforms like call & trade, mobile app and internet.
